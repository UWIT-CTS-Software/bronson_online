Checkerboard
A dynamically-tracked room list that shows what rooms have been checked or are offline.
Seperated by zones, Checkerboard looks through LSM and gathers what rooms have been checked within a certain period of time.

If a room needs to be checked, then it will display red. 
If it has been checked, it will be green. 
And if it is offline, then it will dsiplay grey.

You can filter for rooms that still need to be checked so you don't have to look at all of the rooms on campus at once.
