Tickex
A ticket tool that grabs CTS tickets from TeamDynamix and displays them in Bronson.
Tickex is accurate up to one minute, so tickets appear in realtime.

You can sort by date modified, date created, or the status of the ticket.
There's a search bar that will query ticket details (like keywords in the title, description, ID, and more).

Seperated into three boards (that can have overlapping tickets), 
the new board displays tickets with the status "New" and any tickets that were opened in the past 2 weeks.
The catch all board is all open CTS tickets.
And the closed board is all tickets that have been closed/resolved by CTS.

Clicking on a ticket will open the ticket details popup where you can find more information about the ticket.
If the ticket has changes, the ticket will show you what changed in a little "What Changed" window.

If a ticket is highlighted in yellow, this means the ticket has not been viewed by a technician and needs attention.

Note: There are future plans to be able to interact with the ticket natively within Bronson, but for now
this is a read-only application. To make changes/updates to a ticket, you must go to TeamDynamix 
(which you can find some handy button littered around Tickex to help with this).
