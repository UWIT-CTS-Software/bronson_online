Dashboard


An overview of the various services that Bronson has to offer. 
Lot's of quick lookup information, and some nice-to-know information as well.


Checkerboard Zone Overview
Progress bars of rooms that have been "recently checked" (see CheckerBoard for more info).


Messages
A "message of the day" system that allows Bronson admins to display messages to all technicians.


Room Check Leaderboards
Room check insights where you can see how many rooms a tech has checked within the past 7, 30, and 90 days.


Schedule
A quick overview of technician schedules, including a tab that shows your current weekly schedule. To change your schedule, contact a system admin.


PC Spares
A tracker of where all the CTS spare PCs are on campus.


New CTS Tickets
New tickets that have been pulled from TeamDynamix. Try to keep this list as small as possible!
