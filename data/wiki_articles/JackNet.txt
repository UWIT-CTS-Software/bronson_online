JackNet
A tool for pinging devices across campus and getting their status.

You can choose queries by building and select a device type you want to ping.

The console output will display the Hostname of each device and its IP if available.

If JackNet returns an IP, it will display as green.
If it doesn't, it will display red.
If that device doesn't actually exist in our records, it will not have a color.
